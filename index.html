Our Mission

Hamilton is navigating a period of rapid transformation. Rebuild Hamilton is a resident-led advocacy organization dedicated to ensuring our city’s future is built on smart planning and public accountability. We believe in building a city that works for the people who live here.

Core Principles

Fix What We Own First

Prioritize repairing and modernizing existing infrastructure—pipes, roads, and transit—before expanding the urban boundary.

Own the Infrastructure Debt

End the practice of passing costs to future generations. We must address the $5.2 billion shortfall with honesty and transparency.

Local Jobs, Local Growth

Support procurement and training models that put Hamilton’s skilled, unionized workforce to work rebuilding their own city.

Economic ROI

Every dollar spent on public transit and infrastructure must provide a clear, measurable return on investment for the community.

Support the Movement

Help us fund our research and community advocacy forums for the 2026 municipal election cycle.
